Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped very ripe mango, frozen chopped mango, or canned mango pulp
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 4 teaspoons honey or sugar to taste
  • Dash of ground cardamom, optional
  • Ice, optional

Directions

  1. Add ingredients to the blender
  2. Try to pour it in a cup without spilling it all over the counter
  3. Serve with a sprinkle of cardamom
